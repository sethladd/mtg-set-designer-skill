# Sources

Web references consulted during the creation of the mtg-ip-researcher skill.

## Foundational UB Design Philosophy

- [Designing for Universes Beyond (Rosewater, 2022)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/designing-universes-beyond-2022-09-12) — Eight challenges of UB design: color imbalance, concept misalignment, creature grid gaps, missing mechanics, unexpressible themes, early card lockdown, reprint constraints, stakeholder concerns
- [The Challenges of Making Universes Beyond (Rosewater)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/the-challenges-of-making-universes-beyond) — Doctor Who case study: color mapping, creature grid problems, missing evasion, multi-era scope
- [Designing for the Uninformed (Rosewater)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/designing-for-the-uninformed) — Knowledge pyramid concept: bottom/middle/top tier distribution, lenticular design for IP/non-IP players
- [Designing Universes Beyond (WotC)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/designing-universes-beyond) — UB design framework overview

## Case Studies — Successful UB Products

### Lord of the Rings: Tales of Middle-earth
- [Stepping Into the Ring, Part 3 (Rosewater)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/stepping-into-the-ring-part-3) — Character prioritization: 75 legendary creatures, multiple versions for top-tier characters
- [Crafting the Ring, Part 2 (Rosewater)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/crafting-the-ring-part-2) — Art direction parameters: Epic, Legendary, Storytelling, Pastoral
- [Designing the Lord of the Rings: Tales of Middle-earth (WotC)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/designing-the-lord-of-the-rings-tales-of-middle-earth) — Knowledge pyramid in practice, zero-generic-card standard
- [Creating the One Ring (Rosewater)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/one-ring-to-rule-them-all-2023-06-12) — The Ring Tempts You mechanic as system translation

### Final Fantasy
- [Final Fantasy Vision Design, Part 1 (WotC)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/final-fantasy-vision-design-part-1) — 600+ concept spreadsheet, 5-tier rating system methodology
- [Final Fantasy: A Plethora of Choices (WotC)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/final-fantasy-a-plethora-of-choices) — Scope management across 16 games, 30% franchise-wide elements
- [The Creative Craft of Final Fantasy (WotC)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/the-creative-craft-of-final-fantasy) — 695-page creative development guide, simultaneous English/Japanese development
- [Final Fantasy Shatters Record (Draftsim)](https://draftsim.com/mtg-final-fantasy-legendary-record/) — 200 total legendary creatures, legendary density analysis

### Warhammer 40,000
- [Designing the Warhammer 40,000 Commander Decks (WotC)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/designing-warhammer-40000-commander-decks-2022-09-19) — Color imbalance solution: faction-to-color mapping, visual distinctiveness over lore accuracy

### Doctor Who
- [Designing Magic: The Gathering — Doctor Who (WotC)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/designing-magic-the-gathering-doctor-who) — Audience-era division (not faction-based), two-color Doctors + mono-color Companions structure
- [Doctor Who MTG wants players to 'make their own episode' (Dexerto)](https://www.dexerto.com/magic-the-gathering/mtg-doctor-who-universes-beyond-wants-players-to-make-their-own-episode-2324361/) — Time counter complexity reduction lessons

## Case Studies — UB Failures and Mixed Reception

### Spider-Man
- [The Growing Problem With Universes Beyond (MTGStocks)](https://www.mtgstocks.com/news/20993-the-growing-problem-with-universes-beyond) — Tone mismatch, no Arena support, price collapse
- [Spider-Man Least Excited (The Gamer)](https://www.thegamer.com/magic-the-gathering-mtg-spider-man-universes-beyond-least-excited-fans-have-ever-been/) — Fan disengagement, urban superhero vs. fantasy frame

### TMNT
- [Local Game Stores Comment on MTG's TMNT Reception (Magic Untapped)](https://magicuntapped.com/index.php/news/local-game-stores-comment-on-mtgs-tmnt-reception) — Over 50% negative store reception, failed audience conversion, sales below distributor cost

### Assassin's Creed
- [MTG Assassin's Creed - Who Is It For? (Wargamer)](https://www.wargamer.com/magic-the-gathering/mtg-assassins-creed-who-is-it-for) — Product-audience mismatch, shallow IP mining, cultural moment passed
- [A First Look at MTG Assassin's Creed (WotC)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/announcements/a-first-look-at-magic-the-gathering-assassins-creed) — Scope decisions for multi-era IP
- [Assassin's Creed Will Be a Great UB Set (Card Kingdom Blog)](https://blog.cardkingdom.com/assassins-creed-will-be-a-great-universes-beyond-set/) — Pre-release analysis of IP-to-Magic-plane era mapping
