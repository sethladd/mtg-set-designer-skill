# Sources

Web references consulted during the creation of the mtg-play-designer skill.

## Play Design Structure and Process

- [Wizards: Vision Design, Set Design, and Play Design](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/vision-design-set-design-and-play-design-2017-10-23) — Rosewater explains the restructuring that created Play Design
- [Hipsters of the Coast: Dan Burdick and Play Design](https://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2017/09/kitchen-table-magic-dan-burdick-infinite-universe-play-design/) — Founding of Play Design, team mandate, why Development alone wasn't enough
- [Card Kingdom: Making Magic with Melissa DeTora](https://blog.cardkingdom.com/making-magic-with-melissa-detora/) — How Play Design was structured, what they test for, competitive vs casual
- [MTG Wiki: Play Design](https://mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Play_Design) — Team history, members, process evolution
- [Wizards: Play Design Lessons Learned](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/play-design-lessons-learned-2019-11-18) — Three-mana planeswalker danger, FIRE philosophy consequences, late-change risks
- [Wizards: FIRE Design Philosophy](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/card-preview/fire-it-2019-06-21) — The philosophy that led to the 2019-2020 ban wave
- [Guild Redemund: The FIRE Philosophy of MTG Design](https://blog.guildredemund.net/2021/06/19/the-fire-philosophy-of-mtg-design/) — Analysis of FIRE's impact on card power levels
