# Sources

## Broken Limited Format Analysis

- [Goblin Artisans: Where Did Avacyn Restored Go Wrong?](http://goblinartisans.blogspot.com/2012/08/where-did-avacyn-restored-go-wrong.html) — Loner mechanic failure, Soulbond over-push, weak removal creating bomb-dominated format
- [MTG Salvation: Avacyn Restored Draft](https://www.mtgsalvation.com/forums/the-game/limited-sealed-draft/limited-archives/140049-avacyn-restored-draft) — Limited format breakdown, Black unplayable, Blue-Green dominant
- [Hipsters of the Coast: Why Amonkhet Limited is So Fast](https://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2017/05/amonkhet-limited-fast/) — Exert hyper-aggro, removal in aggro colors, 14-land decks
- [MTG Salvation: Amonkhet is...disappointing](https://www.mtgsalvation.com/forums/the-game/limited-sealed-draft/777468-amonkhet-is-disappointing) — Community analysis of fast format problems
- [Hipsters of the Coast: Evolving Wilds of Battle for Zendikar](https://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2015/11/none-shall-pass-bombs-evolving-wilds-of-battle-for-zendikar/) — Synergy collapse into power-level format
- [MTG Salvation: Battle for Zendikar Postmortem](https://www.mtgsalvation.com/forums/the-game/limited-sealed-draft/658709-battle-for-zendikar-postmortem) — Green worst color, bomb-dependent format
- [MTG Salvation: Rating Theros Block Draft](https://www.mtgsalvation.com/forums/the-game/limited-sealed-draft/560559-rating-theros-block-draft) — Born of the Gods stale mechanics
- [MTG Salvation: Ixalan Draft Format Poll](https://www.mtgsalvation.com/forums/the-game/limited-sealed-draft/784855-poll-whats-your-opinion-on-the-ixalan-draft-format) — Drafts on rails, no pivot points

## Banned Card Autopsies

- [Wizards: Play Design Lessons Learned](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/play-design-lessons-learned-2019-11-18) — Oko development failure, late redesigns, three-mana planeswalker risk, Field of the Dead callback problems
- [Less Practical Magic: The Actual Problem with Oko](https://armlx.blogspot.com/2019/10/the-actual-problem-with-oko-thief-of.html) — Detailed analysis of Oko's +1 elk ability as the real problem
- [DotEsports: Play Design admits Oko was a mistake](https://dotesports.com/mtg/news/magic-the-gathering-play-design-team-admits-oko-was-a-mistake) — Play Design team's public acknowledgment

- [Card Kingdom: Banning of Skullclamp](https://blog.cardkingdom.com/on-this-date-in-magic-history-the-banning-of-skullclamp/) — Late stat change inverting card function
- [Rosewater Blogatog on Skullclamp](https://markrosewater.tumblr.com/post/146336045068/can-you-tell-us-the-story-about-how-skullclamp) — "No one really thought about testing the new version"
- [Wizards: October 2020 B&R Announcement](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/announcements/october-12-2020-banned-and-restricted-announcement) — Omnath banned 18 days after release
- [Card Game Base: Omnath Banned](https://cardgamebase.com/omnath-locus-of-creation-banned-in-standard/) — 72% metagame share, too many abilities on one card
- [MTGGoldfish: Future Future League Failures and Fixes](https://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/future-future-league-failures-and-fixes) — Late-stage changes as highest-risk category
- [Quiet Speculation: R&D's Top 10 Biggest Design Failures](https://www.quietspeculation.com/2011/03/rds-top-10-biggest-design-failures/) — Symmetric effects not fair, historical development errors
