# Sources

Web references consulted during the creation of the mtg-vision-designer skill.

## Vision Design Failure Stories & Retrospectives

- [Lessons Learned, Part 4 (Rosewater)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/lessons-learned-part-4) — Battle for Zendikar: flawed premise, split identity, "I didn't figure out I was making the wrong set"
- [State of Design 2016 (Rosewater)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/making-magic/state-design-2016-2016-08-29) — BFZ above-average complexity, Converge unpopularity
- [State of Design 2014 (Rosewater)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/state-design-2014) — Born of the Gods: second set sacrificed for third, Inspired mechanic failure
- [Born Legacy (Rosewater)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/making-magic/born-legacy-2014-01-20-0) — Born of the Gods design decisions, Tribute replacing Monstrosity
- [Avacyn Restored design analysis (Goblin Artisans)](http://goblinartisans.blogspot.com/2012/08/where-did-avacyn-restored-go-wrong.html) — Loner mechanic failure, Soulbond over-push, weak removal
- [Avacyn Restored design review (PureMTGO)](https://puremtgo.com/articles/design-review-avacyn-restored) — Limited format breakdown analysis
- [More Than Meets the Ikoria (Rosewater)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/making-magic/more-meets-ikoria-2020-04-06) — Two high-maintenance mechanics, "one high-maintenance component" lesson
